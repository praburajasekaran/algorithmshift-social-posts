## ğŸ“± Platform Guidelines

### LinkedIn
- **Character Limit:** 3,000 (use 1,300-1,500 for readability)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant tags
- **Best Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM
- **Image:** Square (1080x1080) or Vertical (1080x1350)
- **Strategy:** Thought leadership, data-driven, professional tone

### Instagram
- **Character Limit:** 2,200 (first 125 characters are critical)
- **Hashtags:** 10-15 relevant tags (30 max, but avoid looking spammy)
- **Best Time:** Monday-Friday, 11 AM - 1 PM
- **Image:** Vertical (1080x1350) for maximum feed impact
- **Strategy:** Visual-first, engaging, inspirational

### Facebook
- **Character Limit:** 63,206 (use 40-80 for best engagement)
- **Hashtags:** 1-3 (less critical on Facebook)
- **Best Time:** Wednesday-Friday, 9 AM - 2 PM
- **Image:** Square (1080x1080)
- **Strategy:** Conversational, community-driven, discussion-focused

### Twitter/X
- **Character Limit:** 280 per tweet (threads recommended)
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 per tweet (3 max)
- **Best Time:** Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 3 PM
- **Image:** Landscape (1600x900)
- **Strategy:** Concise, punchy, thread-worthy content
