## General Design Guidelines

- **Primary Teal:** #11715b (used extensively with glows)
- **Accent Teal:** #1a8c6f (lighter, for gradients)
- **Dark Teal:** #0d5545 (darker, for gradients)
- **Navy:** #0a1628 (professional backgrounds)
- **Deep Blue:** #0d1b2a (futuristic backgrounds)
- **Charcoal:** #1a1a1a (cost comparison, problems)
- **Red (for problems):** #c41e3a (used sparingly)
- **Gold (for premium):** #b8935f (executive touches)

### Glassmorphism Standards
- **Backdrop Blur:** Heavy blur (15-30px radius)
- **Background Opacity:** 8-35% depending on context
- **Borders:** White 2-4px at 25-50% opacity
- **Shadows:** 0px 8px to 0px 20px with 20-40% black opacity
- **Glows:** Teal 0px 10px to 0px 20px with 30-50% opacity

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Hero Stats:** 80-200pt, ExtraBold
- **Headlines:** 36-72pt, ExtraBold
- **Subheads:** 24-44pt, Bold or Medium
- **Body:** 18-32pt, Regular or Medium
- **Labels:** 14-22pt, Regular

### Particle Effects by Post Type
- **Financial (Post 5):** Currency symbols ($), upward arrows, growth indicators
- **Savings (Post 6):** Downward arrows, checkmarks, money saved indicators
- **Empowerment (Post 7):** Lightbulbs, sparkles, innovation symbols
- **Speed (Post 8):** Lightning bolts, motion blur, speed lines, streaks

### Platform Optimization
- **Instagram Feed:** Maximum visual impact, bold stats, mobile-first
- **LinkedIn:** Professional aesthetic, executive credibility, data-driven
- **Twitter:** Fast-read format, immediate comprehension, scroll-stopping
- **Stories:** Immersive, interactive, swipe-up CTAs, maximum effects
